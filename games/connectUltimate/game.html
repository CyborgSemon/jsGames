Connect Ultimate


Welcome to Connect Ultimate. Connect Ultimate is a form of Connect 4, but you get to choose how many connections you need to win.
So if you want to play connect 3, just type 3 down below. If you want to play connect 20 (though it is not recommended) just type 20 down below.
The grid will adapt to the amount of connections you want. So a normal connect 4 game will be a 7 x 6 grid, while connect 5 will have a 8 x 7 grid. (This is why connect 20 isnt recommended)

Enjoy!
