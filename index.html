Welcome to JS Games

Welcome to JS Games. A bunch of board games recreated in JavaScript because why not. This will serve as a demonstration on what you can create with javascript.

To play any of the games, just click on one of the game cards, and it will open in a new tab.

Tic Tac Toe Simple tick tac toe game to play with a friend
Connect 4 Simple connect 4 game to play with a friend
Connect Ultimate Choose how many connections you want, and the grid will adapt
